Aleksey Lobodev

Severodonetsk, 18 y.o., orphan

I was born in Koranit, a village in Lugansk region. I lived with my parents untill I turned 6 y.o. One time they had big fight and after that they got divorced. My father got depressed and because of that when he was drunk he committed suicide. My mother found another man and they started abusing alcohol a lot together. It happened because it was hard to find a job in our village and a lot of people abused alcohol, too. I lived with my mother and father-in-law, we didn�t have food most of the time. After some time my brother and I were hospitalized because we had health problems and after that both of us were sent to a boarding school in Stahanow. Since then I haven't seen my mom and I don't know where she is now. I was 7 when I was sent to the boarding school, but I did not stay there long enough. I was moved to a different boarding school in Severodonetsk. I studies well there and I have been there until this summer. I am thankful that they have good discipline in that school, even though I didn't always followe their rules with my friends. Sometimes we smoked or drank. I want to be a programmer and be successful in my life. Right now I have this opportunity to study in IT Academy Hebron. I hope that I will be good student and will not let people down who is doing this..

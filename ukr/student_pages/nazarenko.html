Vadim Nazarenko

Kakhovka, 20 y.o., orphan

My name is Vadim. I was born in Kherson region. First I lived with my birth mom and grandmother. In 2010 my mother was deprived of her parental rights, my grandmother had health issues and was not able to take care of me. I had 7 grandmother and aunts, but no one did not want to take care of me. Then I was sent to Kakhovka orphanage 'Happiness' where I lived for 5 years and graduated high school. I entered New Kakhovka Polytechnic university as a programmer but after second semester I was dismissed since I missed many classes. After a month I realized I made a huge mistake, but it was too late. I was looking for a job, but there were no results of that as it is almost impossible to find a job for a graduate orphan without any profession. I had no place to stay and went to the village where my childhood friend lived to stay with him. I did not have any luck in developing or finding a job either... So I decided to contact Jim King, a founder of the ministry who supports our orphanage. He directed me to IoT Academy 'Hebron'. At first I doubted, but later I realized that this might be my last chance, a chance to become successful in life!

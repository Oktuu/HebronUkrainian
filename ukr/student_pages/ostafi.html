Vladislav Ostafi

Kherson oblast, 20 y.o., a boy from underprivileged family

I lived in a little town, Skadovsk, where the only sphere developed is tourism. I graduated from Kherson Physical Training Institute. I studies there because I did not have money to study somewhere else. My parents are divorced. I do not see or communicate with my mother since she abuses alcohol heavily. I live with my father who likes to drink as well. They do not help me in any way, therefore, any free time I have I work as a waiter at a local café. While working physically, I realized that it is better to make for living when you have intellectual kind of work. At school I liked applied sciences, therefore when I heard about Hebron project, I realized what a blessing it is for me to be there and in IT sphere in general.

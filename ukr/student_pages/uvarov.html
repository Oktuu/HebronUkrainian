Kiril Uvarov

Severodonetsk, 18 y.o., orphan

My name is Kyril. I was born April 11, 1999. I grew up in my family, I have no brothers and sisters. Dad used to steal and got in prison. The day before my ninth birthday my mother died because of the infection in the body after surgery in the hospital. I understood that when I was hearing my grandmother speaking on the phone about this. It was sad and I cried a lot, started skipping school and learning poorly.The special services came to our home and took me to the shelter, then I was transferred to the boarding school of the Donetsk region. I studied for six years there, from grade 4th until 10th. In 2015, my father died of tuberculosis.Now the Malone family in America sponsors me. They told about Hebron where I study now. In the future, I want to become a mentor for the next Hebron students and to conduct professional master classes. For me the greatest gift is to see a smile of the person you are helping.
